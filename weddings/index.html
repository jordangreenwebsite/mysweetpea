Seasonal, farm-grown wedding flowers for proffesional florists

We supply high-quality, locally grown flowers to florists and designers who want beautiful, seasonal stems without the pressure of growing or sourcing themselves.

What we do

Wedding flower supply
We work alongside florists and floral designers, supplying fresh, seasonal flowers ready for you to condition, design and arrange in your own style.
Our role is simple: We grow and supply the flowers - you create arrangements. 
We do not offer on-the-day wedding floristry, installations or styling. This allows us to focus entirely on providing consistent, high quality flowers you can rely on.

Who this is for

This service is ideal for:
  • Independent wedding florists.
  • Studio florists and floral designers.
  • Florists looking to reduce wholesale imports.
  • Designers wanting locally grown, British flowers.
Whether you're supplying one wedding or multiple dates across the season, we're happy to discuss ongoing supply.

Our Flowers

Seasonal, British & Farm-Grown
All flowers are grown here on the farm and supplied in line with what's naturally in season. This means:
  • Strong, healthy stems.
  • Natural colour pallets.
  • Sustainable growing practices.
  • No air miles or mass imports. 
Flower varieties and colours will vary throughout the year, and we're always happy to discuss seasonal availability in advance. 

How it works

A Simple, Reliable Process
  1. Get in touch to discuss your wedding dates and approximate requirements. 
  2. We confirm seasonal availability and quantities.
  3. Flowers are supplied by bucket or stem count, ready for coniditioning. 
  4. Collection arranged at an agreed time.
No wedding-day involvement, no installations - just dependable flower supply.
