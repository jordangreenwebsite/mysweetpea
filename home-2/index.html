seasonal sweet peas & british flowers

we are a family-run sweet pea flowr farm, growing seasonal british flowers for farm collection, weddings, and florists. our vintage country garden blooms are grown on-site at top barn, newent and harvested at their peak.
almost 40 years of growing and arranging experience in traditional methods and seasonal beauty.

rooted in experience. grown with care.

for nearly four decades, we've been growing and arranging flowers the traditional way - guided by the seasons, the soil, and a love of real country garden blooms. our flowers are grown here at top barn, newent, gloucester without shortcuts, and harvested when they're at their best.

we specialise in sweet peas and heritage varieties chosen for scent, character and natural beauty - the kind of flowers you don't find in mass-produced bouquets.
our amazing work
how it works

our flowers are available by season and by collection only. we don't offer delivery - instead, customers visit the farm to collect flowers at their freshest, straight from the field.

availability changes throughout the year depending on what's in bloom, so we always reccomend checking in advance or following us for seasonal updates.
